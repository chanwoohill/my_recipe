My Favourite Recipe

Pizza

Directions

  • Make the base: Put the flour, yeast, and salt into a large bowl. Pour in water and the oil and bring together until you have a soft, fairly wet dough. Knead for 5 mins until smooth.
  • Make the sauce: Mix the sauce, basil and garlic together, then season to taste.
  • Roll out the dough: roll out the dough into large rounds, about 25cm across, using a rolling pin. Lift the rounds onto two floured baking sheets.
  • Top and bake: Heat oven to 240C. Put another baking sheet or an upturned baking tray in the oven on the top shelf. Scatter with cheese and tomatoes, and season. Bake for 8-10 mins until crisp.
Ingredients

300g strong bread flour

1 tps yeast

1 tps salt

1 tbsp olive oil

100ml tomato sauce

fresh basil

1 garlic clove

125g mozzarella

grated Parmesan

cherry tomatoes
